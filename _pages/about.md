Ching-Chuan (David) Wang is a PhD Candidate in Accounting at the [David Eccles School of Business](https://eccles.utah.edu), [University of Utah](https://www.utah.edu). His research primarily explores areas within financial accounting, with a special emphasis on corporate disclosure and financial reporting.
